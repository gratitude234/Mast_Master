Our Service Portfolio

From green-field builds to lifetime maintenance, we cover every mast & tower need.

What We Do

Tower Fabrication & Erection

Design and build of guyed, self-supporting, monopole and rooftop towers (15 m – 120 m). In-house welding, hot-dip galvanising and on-site erection with certified riggers.

Lightning & Aviation Systems

Installation of lightning arrestors, earthing grids, and ICAO-compliant medium/low-intensity obstruction lights (LED & solar).

Maintenance & Painting

Periodic climb inspections, retightening, guy-wire tensioning, corrosion control, and colour-coded epoxy painting to extend tower life ≥ 25 years.

Dismantling & Relocation

Safe dismantling of obsolete structures, sectional packaging, transport logistics, and precise re-erection at a new site with structural integrity certificates.

Temporary / Mobile Masts

Rapid-deploy steel or aluminium masts (up to 30 m) on trailers – perfect for events, disaster response or coverage testing.

Solar & Electric Street-Light Poles

Design, manufacture and installation of standalone PV or grid-tied street-lights, including luminaire supply and smart controllers.

Our Delivery Process

1
Site Survey & Soil Test

Topography analysis, wind-load data, and geotechnical investigation.

2
Engineering & Design

AutoCAD / STAAD-Pro structural design, PE stamps and client approval.

3
Fabrication & Galvanising

CNC cutting, submerged-arc welding, ISO 1461 hot-dip galvanising.

4
Foundation & Erection

Steel template setting, concrete pouring, tower stacking & guy tension.

5
Commissioning / Handover

As-built drawings, lightning test, integrity report and client sign-off.
