What Happens During an Inspection?

1. Ground Survey

Visual check of foundation, anchors & fencing.

2. Full Climb

Technicians ascend, inspecting every bracing bay.

3. Torque & Tension

Bolts re-torqued; guy wires equalised.

4. Corrosion Control

Rust removal & epoxy recoating where needed.

5. Report & Certificate

Photos, findings & integrity certificate delivered.
